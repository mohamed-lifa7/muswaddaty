Welcome to our powerful online text editor! Whether you're a writer, a developer, or someone who needs to collaborate on documents, our editor is designed to provide you with a seamless and efficient writing experience.

## Key Features

- **Block-based Editor**: Our editor uses a block-based approach, allowing you to organize your content into blocks for easy restructuring and formatting.
- **Rich Text Formatting**: Enhance your writing with various formatting options, including headings, bold, italic, lists, and more.
- **Collaboration**: Invite others to collaborate on your documents in real-time, making it easy to work together on projects or assignments.
- **Cloud Storage**: Your documents are automatically saved and stored in the cloud, ensuring you never lose your work.

## Getting Started

To begin using our online text editor, simply create a new document or open an existing one. You'll be greeted with a clean interface where you can start typing or adding blocks.

### Adding Blocks

To add a new block, click the "+" button at the end of the current block or press the "Enter" key. You can choose from various block types, such as text, code, image, or quote.

### Formatting Text

Highlight the text you want to format and use the toolbar at the top of the editor to apply styles like bold, italic, or different heading levels.

### Collaborating

To collaborate with others, click on `Add Contributor` button and then put the `id` of the contributor. They'll be able to see your changes in real-time, and you'll see theirs.
